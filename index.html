Scott Kirker

Entrepreneur, Developer, Educator, Life Coach

About Scott

Scott Kirker is a Philadelphia-based entrepreneur, full-stack developer, educator, and certified life coach with a unique blend of experience spanning finance, private equity, startups, and education. He is the founder and CEO of Ultimately LLC, a company creating technology products that foster mindful and intentional living, including the apps Grativerse and Ultimately.

Key Projects & Achievements

  • Grativerse: A social gratitude practice app designed to improve mental wellness and community connection.
  • Ultimately: Digital journaling platform encouraging intentional online living.
  • The CouchKeeper: Innovative consumer product solving household issues related to lost items.
  • Educator: Inspiring students at Springside Chestnut Hill Academy in entrepreneurship and coding.

Skills & Expertise

  • Full-Stack Web Development (MEAN Stack, Ionic Framework, Capacitor)
  • Entrepreneurship & Product Development
  • Business Strategy and Operations
  • Life Coaching & Personal Development
  • Education & Mentorship

Personal Values

Scott deeply values mindfulness, intentionality, family, and community. His work reflects his commitment to helping others achieve personal and professional fulfillment through technology and coaching.
